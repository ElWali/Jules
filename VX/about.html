About RQpedia

RQpedia is an open-source project dedicated to providing a comprehensive and accessible library of archaeological sites. Our mission is to democratize access to archaeological data and facilitate research and education in the field.

The data on this site is compiled from a variety of sources and is made available under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License. We encourage you to explore the data, and if you have any questions or would like to contribute, please see our Contribute page.
